Biography

Preetam Dutta is the Co-Founder and CEO of an early-stage AI company focused on building secure machine learning systems for production environments. His work spans machine learning, security, and large-scale deployment.

He received his PhD in Computer Science from Columbia University, where his research focused on machine learning security and adversarial robustness. His work has appeared in top venues including NeurIPS and ICML.

Prior to his doctoral studies, he earned a BS in Computer Science from Yale. He has led production ML efforts across industry and now works at the intersection of research, startups, and real-world AI deployment.
