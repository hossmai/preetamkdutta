Biography

Preetam Dutta is the founder of MarutAI, building a full-stack AI team delivered via a platform for businesses looking to reduce manual workflows or ship AI into their products. He builds for the 99.9% — the people who win through fundamentals, not headlines.

He previously founded and led Elpha Secure, raising $28M+ from institutional investors including Canapi, The Hartford, and AXIS Capital. Across his companies, he has raised $36M+ and invested in dozens across stages, including Discord, Mercury, xAI, OTOY, GrubMarket, and an early investment in Hyzon Motors (public).

A licensed insurance broker, he brings an operator’s lens to risk, security, and scale, and has presented on AI and cybersecurity to U.S. government audiences. He holds a PhD from Columbia University in AI and cybersecurity and is a Yale graduate (Chemical Engineering; Economics & Mathematics).

He also serves as a National Board Member at Schools That Can, supporting access and opportunity through STEM education.
